S.Y. Chen


S.Y. Chen is a lifelong reader and learner, with an intrepid curiosity about the world. She has a Ph.D. in Energy Systems, works as an environmentalist, and is a strong advocate for citizen science. As a writer, she explores the juncture between art and science through careful world-building. As the daughter of a Taiwanese immigrant and a member of the LGBTQ community, she is passionate about positive representation and diverse perspectives. She lives in Colorado with her partner. Her hobbies include wildlife photography, gardening, hiking with her dog and napping with her two perfect cats.
